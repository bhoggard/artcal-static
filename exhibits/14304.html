ART BLOG ART BLOG is extremely pleased to announce the opening of “Can I Get A Witness?” curated by Tisch Abelow, Jashin Friedrich, and Dakotah Savage. This show is the fourth in a series of exhibitions ART BLOG ART BLOG is presenting at a temporary location in Chelsea, NY on the 11th floor of 508 West 26th St. An opening reception will be held on Thursday, July 14th from 6-9 p.m. The exhibition runs through Saturday, July 30th. Open hours will be Wednesdays – Saturdays from Noon- 6p.m. and by appointment.

Mystic Truths are blowing through the air. Listen carefully. Don’t you think it’s time you got everything you desire and more? Maybe, like many of us, you have one foot in the Big Apple and the other stuck in cyberspace…feel like you’re straddling two webs @ once? Well, for a limited time only, you’re in luck: ART BLOG ART BLOG is on. Only one click away -OR- just a short jaunt to gay ol’ 508 W. 26th St. This is blogging gone real time.

So we ask: Do you relate to “AbEx”? Do you enjoy, on occasion, reading? Can you dig that behind every great male artist, there are three female curators?
