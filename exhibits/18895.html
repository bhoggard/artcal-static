MFA Open Studios and Silent Auction

The City College of New York
North Academic Building -- 160 Convent Avenue, New York, NY, (2120 650-8836
Harlem
November 29 - November 29, 2012
Reception: Thursday, November 29, 5 - 9 PM
Web Site

The Studios in Compton-Goethals Hall (room 201) and Shepard Hall (4th floor) will be open to the public, and all of the artists currently in the Studio Art MFA program will be there. Come talk about art, The City College of New York (CUNY), and have some snacks.

In conjunction with the Open Studio there will be a silent auction of new artwork by the MFA students, benefitting the Graduate Art Society, a student organization which brings artists, curators and other art professionals to lecture to the CCNY community.
