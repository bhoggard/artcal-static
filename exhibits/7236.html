Temporary Toy Theater Museum

St. Ann's Warehouse
38 Water Street, 718.254.8779
DUMBO
May 23 - May 31, 2008
Reception: Friday, May 23, 5 - 7 PM
Web Site

Mounted throughout the Warehouse, the finest examples of historical and contemporary toy theaters, representing 100 artists and collectors.

Opening Reception: Friday May 23rd from 5:00 to 7:00pm; FREE! The exhibit will be open (and free) to the public and ticketholders one hour before showtimes and starting at 2:00pm on May 29 and 30.
