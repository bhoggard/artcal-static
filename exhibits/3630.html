Dirty Hands, A Group Print Show

Cinders Gallery
28 Marcy Avenue, 718-388-2311
Williamburg
December 13, 2006 - January 11, 2007
Reception: Thursday, December 14, 6 - 10 PM
Web Site

Featuring Julien Langendorff, Mark Todd, Esther Pearl Watson, Leif Goldberg,Erin Rosenthal, Swoon, Neil Burke, Andrew Jeffrey Wright, Edie Fake,Sam McPheeters, Xander Marro, Marc Bell, Saelee Oh, Souther Salazar, Caroline Hwang, Kelie Bowman, Shawn Reed, Steak Mtn, Onya Hogan Finlay, Ryan Jacob Smith, Anders Nilsen, Rachell Sumpter, Logan Macdonald, Kim Schifino, Eric Shaw, Monica Canilao, Ian Vanek, Celeste Toogood, Natalja Kent, James McShane, Kyle Ranson, Jo Dery, Jacob Mickelson, Allyson Mellberg, Jeremy Taylor, Justin Williams, Mike Taylor, Sto, Fionnuala Ross, Ginger Brooks Takahashi and Bongout.

Dirty Hands focuses on a current group of artists that use various forms of printmaking as a viable contemporary medium, including screenprints, etchings, stencils, wood block prints and artist books. The immediacy and accessibility of printmaking creates a medium that can be mass-produced in editions, artist books, and posters.The process of hand-printing also adds the distinction of the human hand at work, revealing its slight imperfections and labor to be refreshing in our increasingly digital age.
