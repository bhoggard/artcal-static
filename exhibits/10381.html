2nd Annual 13th Hour

Last Rites Gallery
511 West 33rd Street , 3rd floor, 212-529-0666
Hell's Kitchen
October 24 - November 22, 2009
Reception: Saturday, October 24, 7 - 11 PM
Web Site

On October 24th, Last Rites Gallery presents it’s second annual 13th Hour group exhibit, celebrating the Halloween season. The show features artists from around the globe, working in an array of mediums including painting, photography, sculpture, and film. Among the talent included in this exhibit is Chet Zar, who’s work has been described by TOOL’s Adam Jones as “A modern twist crashing into a classical approach. Beautiful and scary”; David Stoupakis, who’s Gothic surrealistic work has been seen on KORN’s album covers along with others; Shawn Barber, who, along with lecturing about painting globally, has shown his dark, tattoo-inspired works on canvas around the world. From gothic elegance to finely crafted grotesquery, the beauty within the darkness is embraced and brought into the spotlight. The spirit of Halloween has never looked so good.

Participating artists include: Shawn Barber, Matthew Bone, Paul Booth, Vincent Castiglia, Andy B. Clarkson, Christopher Conte, Coro, Brendan Danielsson, Ewelina Ferruso, Fred Harper, Stephanie Henderson, Robert Hernandez, Kris Kuksi, Craig LaRotonda, Jeff McMillan, Gilbert Oh, Pooch, Anthony Pontius, Dan Quintana, Celeste Rappone, Paul Rumsey, Martina Secondo Russo, David Stoupakis, Tin, Brian Viveros, Jasmine Worth, Genevive Zacconi and Chet Zar.
