Works on Paper NYC: Juried Exhibition

Jeffrey Leder Gallery
2137 45th Road, 3rd Floor, 212-924-8944
Long Island City
June 24 - July 15, 2012
Reception: Sunday, June 24, 5 - 7 PM
Web Site

From drawing, painting, printmaking to photography, even sculpture, works on paper is a varied, exciting and versatile medium. This exhibition celebrates the range of media that paper can facilitate. These pieces incorporate a variety of genres. Whether working in abstraction or realism, non-objective or figurative, although the artists utilize diverse techniques they are alike in their ability to employ paper to satisfy their artistic passion.
