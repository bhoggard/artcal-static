Cultural Hysteria

Chashama 266 Gallery
266 West 37th Street, 212-391-8151 x 26
Midtown
July 21 - August 6, 2011
Reception: Saturday, July 23, 2 - 6 PM
Web Site

The artist will be working live in the gallery, creating new site works. Random acts of recorded sounds and acoustic live music will also be performed by the artist.

Opening reception: Saturday, July 23, 2-6pm Poetry reading by Patricia Murphy: Saturday, July 30, 5pm. “Ekphrasis”

Referencing the overwhelming deluge of information, problems posed by pollution and population and the frantic pace of of life in the 21st century. New works dealing with death, dreams, imagination, relationships, attitude and evolution.

Artist Bio: Mario Zecca was born in Oklahoma, grew up in the central Bronx, spent the following 20 years living in Oklahoma-attending OU; and California, experiencing a wide array of occupational activities from street musician and book illustrator to shoveling cement for house foundations. Drawn to comic book art and drawing from an early age, he has exhibited fine art, careered in illustration and design and made major forays into writing, music and miniature sculpture.
