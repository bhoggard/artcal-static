Paul Graham

Greenberg Van Doren Gallery
730 Fifth Avenue, 212-445-0444
Midtown
March 18 - May 2, 2009
Web Site

Greenberg Van Doren Gallery and Salon 94 Freemans are pleased to present PAUL GRAHAM: Photographs 1981-2006. The show is on view at Salon 94 Freemans from March 12 – April 18, 2009 and is on view at Greenberg Van Doren from March 18 – May 2, 2009. Both shows run concurrently with Graham’s solo show at The Museum of Modern Art, New York, PAUL GRAHAM: a shimmer of possibility, on view from February 4 – May 28, 2009. A shimmer of possibility is comprised of multi-part as well as singles images captured by Graham during his travels across America. Scenes of pedestrian moments are woven together to create quiet yet compelling vignettes that are punctuated with glimpses of (candid human emotion, and the often over looked beauty found within the everyday American landscape.

SteidlMACK is also publishing the eponymously titled book Paul Graham, to coincide with a major survey of Graham’s work at Museum Folkwang, Essen, Germany.
