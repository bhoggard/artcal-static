Exhibition of Works by Newly Elected Members and Recipients of Honors and Awards

The American Academy of Arts and Letters
633 West 155th Street, Audubon Terrace, Broadway between 155 and 156 Streets, 212-368-5900
Harlem
May 18 - June 10, 2007

The American Academy of Arts and Letters is pleased to announce its exhibition of paintings and sculpture; architecture photographs, models and renderings; original manuscripts; and musical scores, by newly elected members and recipients of honors and awards.

Paintings, sculpture, works on paper, architecture models and renderings by

Newly Elected Members in Art and Architecture Robert Irwin · Billie Tsien

Architecture Award Winners Wes Jones · Tom Kundig · Eric Owen Moss · Lebbeus Woods

Art Award Winners Sally Hazelet Drummond · Jackie Gendel · Juan Gomez · Julian Hatton · Bryan Hunt Sarah Oppenheimer · Dana Schutz

Art Purchases Clytie Alexander · Robert Bordo · Sally Hazelet Drummond · Mark Ferguson · Jackie Gendel · Juan Gomez · Warren Isensee · Saul Leiter · Sally Mann · Stephen Mueller · Emily Nelligan · Ann Pibal · Cynthia Westwood · Emna Zghal

Original manuscripts and personal correspondence by Newly Elected Members in Literature Deborah Eisenberg · Mary Gordon · Allan Gurganus · Jim Harrison · Harper Lee · Annie Proulx

Literature Award Winners Joan Acocella · Charles Baxter · Jez Butterworth · Charles D’Ambrosio · Junot Diaz · Tony D’Souza · Barbara Ehrenreich · Amy Hempel · Suji Kwock Kim · Sarah Manguso · David Markson · Robert Morgan · Adam Rapp · Joan Silber · Dana Spiotta · William T. Vollmann · Dean Young

Musical scores by Newly Elected Member in Music Steven Stucky

Music Award Winners Leonardo Balada · Mason Bates · Chester Biscardi · Ben Johnston
