Carriage Trade
62 Walker Street, 212 343 2944
Tribeca / Downtown
April 14 - April 17, 2010
Reception: Saturday, April 17, 6 - 10 PM
Web Site

carriage trade benefit Saturday, April 17, from 6–10 pm, Raffle will begin at 7:30pm

Artwork will be available for preview: Wednesday, April 14, from 2–6pm Thursday, April 15, from 2–6pm Friday, April 16, from 2–6pm Saturday, April 17, from 12–6pm, Raffle: 6–10pm

carriage trade is re-opening as a non-profit at 62 Walker Street (near Broadway) with a benefit to raise funds for its upcoming programming. Tickets for the raffle are $125 and can be purchased through our website (www.carriagetrade.org) and in the gallery the week of the benefit. The number of tickets sold will equal the number of donated works. On the night of the raffle, ticket holders are entitled to choose an artwork once their numbers have been randomly drawn. The artworks will be presented anonymously, with the identity of the artist revealed only after the ticket holder acquires the piece.
