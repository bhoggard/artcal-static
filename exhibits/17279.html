INQUIRY curated by Austin Thomas /Two Coats of Paint @ Bushwick Open Studios

Two Coats of Paint
117 Grattan Street, No. 419, (860) 460-0055
Bushwick/Ridgewood
June 1 - June 3, 2012
Reception: Saturday, June 2, 4 - 7 PM
Web Site

To celebrate the fifth anniversary of her art blog, Two Coats of Paint, Sharon Butler invited Austin Thomas of Pocket Utopia, one of the first artist-run spaces in Bushwick (recently reopened on Henry Street on the Lower East Side), to curate a painting exhibition in Two Coats’s new Bushwick HQ. Artists selected include Margaret Atkinson, Guy Corriero, Lawrence Greenberg, and Edie Nadelhaft.

Hours: Friday, June 1, 6-8pm, Saturday, June 2, and Sunday, June3, 12-7pm.

Artists’ Reception on Saturday, June 2, 4pm.
