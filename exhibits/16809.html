17th Annual Benefit and Raffle

Momenta Art
56 Bogart Street, 718-218-8058
Bushwick/Ridgewood
April 6 - April 25, 2012
Reception: Friday, April 6, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

The opening reception for the Preview Exhibition will be on Friday, April 6th from 6 – 9 pm The Benefit and Raffle Drawing Party will be on Wednesday, April 25th from 6 – 10pm

Momenta Art presents its 17th annual Spring Benefit to support its ongoing mission to exhibit the work of emerging and underrepresented artists. A ticket guarantees you a work of art and entrance for two to the raffle-drawing party held at 56 Bogart Street on April 25th. Raffle tickets may be purchased until all the tickets are sold and are limited to the number of artworks available. Tickets will be offered at a pre-sale rate through April 7th.

Momenta’s 2012 Spring Benefit will present work by both emerging and known artists. The complete list of participating artists will be announced on April 6th. The work will be on view at Momenta for three weeks prior to the raffle drawing party from April 6th to 25th.

On April 25th, the gallery will be open for preview from 12 to 6. The raffle drawing will begin at 7:30 pm and will continue until all works have been offered, with the first randomly drawn ticket giving its holder first choice of one work displayed.

As a not-for-profit exhibition organization, Momenta depends on the contributions of people like you. We sincerely thank all participating artists, event sponsors, committee members, and ticket buyers for their generous support.

More info and tickets at www.momentaart.org/benefit12
