For immediate Release: Camel Art Space Presents:

• On the Grid •

August 21 – August 29, 2010 2 Weekends only: 12 – 6 pm or by appointment Opening reception: August 21, 6pm – 10pm Location: 722 Metropolitan Avenue, Brooklyn NY 11237 Directions: L – train to Graham Avenue

An invitational show comprised of artists that have submitted work for consideration by Camel Art Space over the past year. These artists have been invited to submit work to be placed on a grid formed on the walls of Camel Art Space. The artwork’s location on the grid will be randomly determined by lottery.

Denying itself the empowered role of curator, Camel Art Space has left the contents of the show to the chance doings of the artists involved, making a cohesive, centralized concept or theme impossible. Thematic chaos promises to reign within the structured confines of the grid, celebrating the unexpected by giving the artist control and allowing the prospect of chance to arrange the work. Within this rigid predictable structure no one knows what will happen…

(non)-curated by: Chris McGee & Rob de Oude

Camel Art Space is an Artist operated exhibition Space with a focus on current issues in art within a not for profit work frame, is an affiliate member of Williamsburg Gallery Association and is participating in 2:nd Friday Art Walk.
