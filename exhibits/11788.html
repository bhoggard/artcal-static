Statuesque

City Hall Park
City Hall Park
Tribeca / Downtown
June 24 - December 3, 2010
Web Site

Public Art Fund will offer free weekly docent tours of its current exhibition in City Hall Park, Statuesque. Designed to provide visitors with an overview of the exhibition’s themes as well as details about each artist and the works included, the tours will visit each of the ten figurative sculptures on view and last approximately 30 minutes. Tours occur each Thursday through the exhibition’s closing December 3, 2010 (weather permitting) and originate at the south side of the fountain near Huma Bhabha’s “The Orientalist” at 12:30pm.

To sign up for a tour, please email [email protected] or arrive at the south side of the fountain five minutes before the tour is scheduled to begin. Groups of eight or more should contact Public Art Fund in advance.
