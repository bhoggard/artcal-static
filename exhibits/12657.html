NEW YEAR, NEW WORK, NEW FACES

STOREFRONT
16 Wilson Avenue, ground floor
Bushwick/Ridgewood
January 1 - January 23, 2011
Reception: Saturday, January 1, 1 - 3 PM
Web Site

BUSHWICK, BROOKLYN—Join gallery directors Jason Andrew and Deborah Brown as we open our next year of exciting exhibitions at Storefront in Bushwick. Hot off a fresh review in The New York Times, the gallery hosts an exciting new show in the new year featuring new work by a new group of rising art stars. The exhibition can be previewed at a fundraiser for the Bushwick based non-profit NORTE MAAR, on Tuesday, December 28, 7-9PM. $20, beer + pizza. The official opening reception will a brunch held on New Years Day, January 1, from 1PM-3PM. Exhibition continues through January 23. Storefront hours and directions can be found at www.storefrontbk.com

NEW YEAR, NEW WORK, NEW FACES: Ali Aschman * John Avelluto * Gregg Baker * Yevgeniya Baras * Myles Bennett * Jim Butler * Maria Calandra * Paul Campbell * Ceclie Chong * Henry Chung * Amanda Church * Elisabeth Condon * Kevin Curran * Mila Dau * Blane De St. Croix * Kylie Heidenheimer * Cooper Holoweski * Leslie Kerby * Wendy Klemperer * Lars Kremer * Matthew Langley * Mary Kate Maher * Karen Marston * Loren Munk * Carol Salmanson * Rob Servo * Julie Torres * Rhonda Tymeson * Don Voisine * Robert Walden * Chuck Webster * Josh Willis * Mark Weathers * and others.
