Dead in August is pleased to present the third installment of painting, sculpture, photography, and video. Exhibiting Artists: Adam Henry, Adam Parker Smith, Beau Chamberlain, Brian O’Connell, Brent Birnbaum, Carolyn Salas, Cary Whittier, Catherine Czacki, Chris Dorland, Christian Gordy, Colin Michael Simmons, Eddie Chu, Emily Mae Smith, Jacob Hansen, Jason Peters, Jen Dohne, Jesse Lindmar, Joe Graham-Felsen, Joshua Abelow, Justin Bereman, Kyle Simon, Louis Cameron, Mat Bushell, Marina Kappos, Maureen Cavanaugh, Michael Alan, Nicholas Knight, Owen Rundquist, Paul Jacobsen, Salome Oggenfuss, Scott Calhoun, Scott Ogden, Susan Hunt, and Wayne Adams. The exhibition will run through September 20.

Dead in August is a Brooklyn community driven month of events encompassing the performing arts, fine arts, food and design. The project is organized by Meaghan Kent and inspired by the space itself, The Pentagon, Brooklyn. With great thanks to Susan and Howard Hunt of The Pentagon.

General exhibition space hours to be posted on the website. Also open by appointment. For further information, please contact Meaghan Kent: [email protected]
