DEPARTURE//AMANDA MARSALIS//THAYER GOWDY

Ed. Varie (East 7th Street)
208 East 7th Street
East Village / Lower East Side
June 9 - June 30, 2011
Reception: Thursday, June 9, 7 - 10 PM
Web Site

Ed. Varie is pleased to announce Departure, an exhibition of polaroid photography by Amanda Marsalis and Thayer Allyson Gowdy. Both Gowdy and Marsalis are based in California and this exhibition will mark their gallery debut in Manhattan.

As an accompaniment to the show, a limited edition hand-sewn SX-70 camera case has been crafted in collaboration between Ed.Varie and MAKR Carry Goods, as well as limited edition zines and museum quality print sets will also be released at the opening.

Ed. Varie is located in the heart of the East Village//Alphabet City with a rich history in the arts and community. Ed. Varie presents artists of all backgrounds and media from North America, Europe, Canada and Australia.
