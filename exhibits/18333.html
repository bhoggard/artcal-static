Chashama Harlem Open Studios

Chashama 461 Gallery
461 West 126th street, New York, NY
Harlem
October 6 - October 7, 2012
Web Site

FREE and open to the public, chashama will be hosting our Harlem Open Studios in conjunction with the Harlem Art Walk Tour (HAWT) on October 6 – 7, 2012. Visitors can explore over 30 artist studios throughout 2 floors of our West Harlem location and a group show curated by Marcela Carvalho. From 12pm to 6pm, on both Saturday and Sunday, guests can tour the studios, interact with chashama’s artists and learn more about chashama and our roster of innovative arts programs.

Mark your calendars and don’t miss this amazing event!

Participating artists: Babatunde Ajiboye, Elizabeth Allison, Aleathia Brown, Marcela Carvalho, Roc Cayard, Elvira Clayton, Katherine L.
