Resilient – Recent paintings & subway signs by COPE2

Dorian Grey Gallery
437 East 9th Street, 516-244-4126
East Village / Lower East Side
May 17 - June 24, 2012
Reception: Thursday, May 17, 5 - 8 PM
Web Site

For over 30 years, COPE 2 has steadily risen to become one of the world’s most prolific and recognizable living aerosol artists on the graffiti art scene. From high fashion and sports apparel to popular video games, COPE 2 ‘s art work has crossed over to popular culture. A self–taught artist, born and raised in the south Bronx, his paintings have graduated from the subway cars of the IRT line and now grace the walls of galleries and private collections throughout the world. Today his distinctive style has evolved to incorporate classic urban graffiti style “bubble letters” interwoven with intricate abstract compositions.

Dorian Grey Gallery proudly presents Resilient, an exhibition featuring recent paintings & unique works on paper by COPE 2.

Please join us on May 17th from 5-8pm as we celebrate an artist that successfully bridges the worlds of New York’s gritty street culture and fine art.
