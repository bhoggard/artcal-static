CALENDAR | HOSTING

Fragmentation of the Self: Smeared, Smudged, Marked and Drawn

Rush Arts Gallery
526 West 26th Street, Suite 311, 212 691 9552
Chelsea
March 30 - May 27, 2006
Reception: Thursday, March 30, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Participating Artists: Nina I. Buxenbaum, Saul Chernick, Erica Eyres, Lynn Palewicz, Nathaniel M. Quinn, Mary A Valverde, Haeri Yoo

This exhibition focuses on the way that seven artists utilize the language of drawing and the idea of fragmentation as a means to understand and communicate ideas about the self.

Identity is a physical and metaphysical construct that exists in a constant state of change. Delineated markers of identity such as culture, ethnicity, spirituality, sexuality, beauty and gender are often smeared, smudged, severed, drawn, erased, and drawn again. Nina Buxenbaum, Saul Chernick, Erica Eyres, Lynn Palewicz, Nathaniel Mary Quinn, Mary Valverde, and Haeri Yoo each utilize various mediums and sub-themes to represent this perpetual state. – Guest Writer – Rose Oluronke Ojo

Concepts, Process, Materials: An Artist Talk: Join the exhibiting artists and Curatorial Director in conversation Saturday May 6, 2006, 4 – 6pm
