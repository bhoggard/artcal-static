Hybrid Life Forms Group Exhibition

Opening Reception: Saturday February 19th, 7-9pm Open hours on Sunday, February 20th: 12-6pm Feb 19th & 20th, 2011 ...... Curated by Jiyoun Lee-Lodge Location: 174 Franklin Av, Greenpoint, Brooklyn, NY

Artists: Linda Bernal, Aidan Sofia Earle, Benjamin T. Edmiston, Megan Hays, Gregory Hayes, Jiyoun Lee-Lodge, Rachel Meuler, Hector Madera-Gonzalez, Colette Robbins, Ingrid Staats, Jonathan Torres

Hosts: Candace Goodrich, Lau Gallico, Giancarlo Romero

600/3×9 Project presents Hybrid Life Forms, a group exhibition of emerging artists curated by Jiyoun Lee-Lodge. Utilizing material and form, the works of these artists respond visually to rapid globalization, advancing technology and each, in their own way, seek to describe the shock waves that occur at the intersection of the present and future. The question of hybridization is addressed in these works as if material and concept were DNA, allowing the artists to willfully create their own particular mutations.
