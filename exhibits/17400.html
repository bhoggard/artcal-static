Peter Dugovic “City Life Signs”

Clic Gallery
255 Centre Street, 212-219-9308
Tribeca / Downtown
June 14 - July 1, 2012
Reception: Thursday, June 14, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Clic is pleased to present City Life Signs, the newest series of paintings from Maine-based artist Peter Dugovic. Drawing on his professional experience as a sign-painter, coppersmith, and construction worker, Dugovicâ€™s close-up, mechanically-detailed studies of neon signage are paeans to retro urban Americana. By zooming in on small portions of signs from slanted perspectives, Dugovic creates vibrant and electrically-charged microcosms that course alive with the rhythms of the American city. City Life Signs will be on view from June 14th to July 1st with the opening reception taking place on June 14th from 6-8 pm.
