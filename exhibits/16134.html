Seong Kyung Hee Solo Exhibit

ArtGate Gallery
520 West 27th Street, No. 101, 646-455-0986 / 646-455-0989
Chelsea
February 7 - February 25, 2012
Reception: Thursday, February 9, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Seong Kyung Hee’s abstract paintings are based on the changes endured in her life and convey her demure ideology. She believes in a “silent” creation whereby the artist’s vibrant imaginations are stripped from the canvas, leaving a sedate painting in which the interpretation lies with the viewer alone.

Seong Kyung Hee received a BFA from the College of Fine Arts at Seoul National University, where she won the Chief Justice Award. She went on to receive a MFA from the Graduate School of Fine Arts at Seoul National University. She has exhibited in numerous solo and group shows around Korea for over twenty years. She is currently a lecturer in the Oriental Paintings department at the College of Arts, Seoul National University.
