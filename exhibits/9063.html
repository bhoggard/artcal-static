Asian Ink Masters

Ethan Cohen Fine Arts
18 Jay Street, between Hudson and Greenwich, 212-625-1250
Tribeca / Downtown
March 11 - April 30, 2009
Reception: Wednesday, March 11, 7 - 9 PM
Web Site

Ethan Cohen Fine Arts presents two concurrent exhibitions featuring several prominent Asian contemporary artists and a discussion with the artist Qin Feng and Ethan Cohen on the innovations in contemporary ink will be held at the gallery the evening of March 11th at 7pm.

Asian Ink Masters opening March 11th, honors eight Asian ink masters who exemplify innovation and tradition in calligraphy and ink painting. These renowned artists include Wenda Gu, Qin Feng, Wang Dongling, Naoto Nakagawa, Ma Desheng, Huang Zhiyang, Qiu Zhijie, and the late C.C.Wang. While paying homage to the long tradition of calligraphy and landscape painting, this exhibition highlights a diverse group of Asian artists who are at the forefront of experimentation in ink painting. The show challenges the viewer to see the larger evolution and the most current developments in ink painting over the course of 25 years.
