ABOUT MIGUEL CORDERA http://www.cordera.com.mx/

I paint as a way to understand my surroundings, myself. Most of my work has been autobiographical and is often related to altered states of consciousness. My work consists of acrylic on canvas, painted in a medium scale and is often created as a related series of multiples.The execution of the work is done by layering different forms to create a sensation of depth and chromatic balance with an euphoric touch that departs from geometry and gets closer to ornamental design. Through this series I relate to modern painting and other contemporary artists that work deeply with color.
