The End is Nigh

Higher Pictures
980 Madison Avenue, 212-249-6100
Upper East Side
April 14 - May 19, 2007
Reception: Saturday, April 14, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Far ranging in its approach and genre, this salon style photographic exhibition contains subtly divergent notions of ends. War, an individual action, a conversation, life, liberty, culture… Examples include: Harry Callahan, Eleanor, c.1950; Diane Arbus, Boy With a Straw Hat Waiting to March in a Pro-War Parade, 1967; Boogie, Sagrada Familia (Church of the Holy Family), 2007.
