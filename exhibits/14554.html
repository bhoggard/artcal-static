Try2.1 Art Show and Silent Auction

Jen Bekman Gallery
6 Spring Street, between Elizabeth St. and Bowery, 212-219-0166
East Village / Lower East Side
August 27 - August 27, 2011
Reception: Saturday, August 27, 7 - 9 PM
Web Site

Please join us for the second annual Try2 Art Show and Silent Auction benefiting The Leukemia and Lymphoma Society to help stop leukemia, lymphoma and Hodgkin’s disease. The one night event will feature artwork from local and national artists with opening bids starting at $50. All funds raised will go to The Leukemia and Lymphoma society making it a fun night to pick up a good deal and help a great cause.

For more information or to view the latest donated artwork and read about the artists, please go to www.trybenefitartshow.blogspot.com
