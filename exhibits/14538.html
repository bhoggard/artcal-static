TONNEOVEFUNNE

et al Projects
416 West 145th Street, 914-498-8328
Harlem
August 19 - September 2, 2011
Reception: Friday, August 19, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

et al Projects is pleased to invite you to attend our inaugural exhibition TONNEOVEFUNNE featuring new works by Devon Dunhill Clapp and Brian Galderisi, opening on August 19th, 2011 from 6-9pm. These two artists explore the duality between themes that at first glance seem to be opposite but when examined within the cyclical order of the natural world they are complimentary to each other. Throughout human existence these phenomenons have been related to ritual and cognitive behavior and perpetuated by societal trends.

et al Projects is an artist and curatorial collective space (not for profit and non-incorporated presently) that seeks to create a multi-disciplinary project space to foster a dialogue amongst a diverse group of creative individuals. We look forward to meeting and working with artists, poets, performers, writers, et al. All are welcome to propose an idea for an exhibition, installation or an event. We are dedicated to creating an environment where all types of work can be shown.

For more information contact us at 914-498-8328 or email [email protected] et al Projects is located at 416 W. 145th street, New York, NY, 10031. (lower level look for the lanterns on the gate and enter through the lower street entrance level), around the corner from City College of New York. Take the A,C,B,D to the 145th street stop.
