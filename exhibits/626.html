Pat Steir, Moon Paintings and a River

Cheim & Read
547 West 25th Street, 212-242-7727
Chelsea
March 31 - May 7, 2005
Reception: Thursday, March 31, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Pat Steir’s paintings have been exhibited since 1964, including several Whitney and Venice biennales. She represented the U.S. in the 19th Sao Paolo Bienal and was also included in Documenta IX.

In “Moon Paintings and a River,” Steir explores an idea of visual space that is at once flat and deep in her singular painting language. This exhibition will feature the monumental “Blue River,” a twenty six-foot painting on canvas in which an enormous expanse of blue is enclosed by vibrant red and silver. In the “Moon Paintings,” Pat Steir invents a dialogue between vast fields of subtly modulating color and singular, definitive gestures. The gestures and events in the paintings hover and emerge out of a mist of thrown and poured rivulets.
