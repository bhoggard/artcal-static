ESP TV 2

LOUIS V E.S.P. at The Schoolhouse
330 Ellery Street, Floor 3, 732-552-7199
Bushwick/Ridgewood
May 6 - May 6, 2011
Reception: Friday, May 6, 8 - 3 AM
Web Site

NOTE: THIS EVENT DOES NOT TAKE PLACE AT LOUIS V E.S.P. HQ, BUT AT THE SCHOOLHOUSE, 330 ELLERY ST. BK, NY

ESP TV #2 taping scheduled for May 6th at The Schoolhouse with performances by :

FORMA (http://www.formasounds.com/) INNERGAZE (http://www.myspace.com/innergaze) ANA ROMAN w/ MATTHEW CARON (http://www.analolaroman.com/?page_id=359) MAZING VIDS (http://mazingvids.com/) VICTORIA KEDDIE (http://victoriakeddie.com/) and “The Dust Up” with SAM MICKENS and a panel of distinguished guests.

with the (un)usual onslaught of the video arts… Videos by KAREN CHAN, LILY BENSON, ANGIE BERMUDA, DANA BELL, JASON UNDERHILL and more!

Hosted by SAM MICKENS (Sam Mickens Ecstatic Showband Revue, The Dead Science, Xiu Xiu) and produced by LOUIS V E.S.P.

Immediate afterparty with DJ’s SHAWN O’SULLIVAN (Led Er Est/Further Reductions) and MATTHEW RADUNE (The Wilding Corporation)

ESP TV is a showcase/collage of performance and video based art simultaneously taped and mixed “in front of a live studio audience” with live greenscreening and video manipulation. All of the night’s events are sent to a VHS deck and the tape is cut down into 28 minute bite sized pieces before being sent to Manhattan Neighborhood Network for airing.

Please visit www.louisvesp.com for more information on this and other events/exhibitions. Contact: Scott Kiernan 732-552-7199
