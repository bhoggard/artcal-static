Christina Vantzou: Nature Collection

MonkeyTown
58 North 3rd Street, between Kent and Wythe, 718-384-1369
Williamburg
May 26 - July 9, 2006
Web Site

She has been drawing for as long as she can remember. Over time, her drawings have evolved into animations and abstract videos. In recent years, Christina has been active in projects encompassing fine art, illustration, graphic design, motion graphics, video, animation, and music. Recent commercial work has included creating a TV identity and title design for Mtv Netherlands, and a making a promotional video for the band Interpol. She also enjoys the luxury of devoting time to her own various projects. In 2004, Christina Vantzou began an audiovisual collaboration with composer Adam Wiltzie under the name, The Dead Texan. Together they released a cd and dvd containing dreamy little videos which are blissfully synchronized to ambient, classically-inspired music. The Dead Texan conducted a series of live performances across Europe throughout 2005 and are currently working on a follow up cd/dvd release on Kranky Records.

For Monkey Town, Christina has designed a 4 channel video installation pairing hand drawn artwork animated frame by frame with abstract video backgrounds. The piece stems from a growing collection of nature imagery around Christina’s studio and a longing for Spring. Inspired by the aesthetics of traditional Japanese screen painting, Christina has assembled a series of videos that form a panoramic landscape and is enhanced by subtle animation, color, and sounds.
