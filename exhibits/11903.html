COCA Center for Outdoor Contemporary Art & Open Space Alliance for North Brooklyn present the first annual

Wet Summer Arts Festival

TIZIANA AGNELLO, JASON ROBERT BELL & MARNI KOTAK, GULSEN CALIK, BEN GODWARD, REBECCA HERMAN & MARK SHOFFNER, LIZ-N-VAL, MUSA, MOLLY LOWE, YO PARK, JEFFREY ALLEN PRICE, CHRISTY SINGLETON, OONA STERN CIGDEM TANKUT, NICOLE TSCHAMPEL

CURATED BY ELLIOT LESSING

SATURDAY AUGUST 28, 11 AM – 6 PM

McCarren Park, BROOKLYN

RAINY DAY PLAN: SUNDAY AUG 29, 11 AM – 6PM

Feeling hot?

THIS SUMMER, New York’s most sizzling artists bring back THE COOL for a day-long exhibition of FRESHLY-MADE OUTDOOR SCULPTURE – where the NEWEST waves in Contemporary Art crash with the inspiring sublime beauty of WATER!

ALL 15 Wet artists will be on hand to host their HOTTEST & WETTEST VISIONS, deliver insights regarding their newest creations and provide a welcoming spirit throughout 3 areas of Brooklyn’s very own McCarren Park

Art lovers, Park-goers and City Visitors of all-ages are invited to rediscover their park as a LIVING OUTDOOR MUSEUM where Art, Nature & the Spirit of Public Life unite in a colorful whirlpool of DISCOVERY & ADVENTURE!
