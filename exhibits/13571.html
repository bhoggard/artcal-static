Established in 2009 between Paris and London, Guest House Gallery has no permanent address. GHG finds unusual urban playgrounds in which to organize one-night art shows in big cities across the globe. GHG introduces emerging artists to an international network of collectors. GHG encourages the production of side-projects. GHG shares a simple and affordable pleasure : love and purchase artworks <
