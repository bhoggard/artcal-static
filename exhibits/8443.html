Duck Soup

La Mama Gallery
6 East 1st Street
East Village / Lower East Side
November 21 - December 21, 2008
Reception: Friday, November 21, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

CURATED BY DAPHNE FITZPATRICK

“Take two turkeys, one goose, four cabbages, but no duck, and mix them together. After one taste, you’ll duck soup for the rest of your life.” – Groucho Marx

La Galleria is pleased to present a group exhibition curated by artist Daphne Fitzpatrick entitled Duck Soup. The exhibition title refers to both the Marx Bros. film and the American slang use of the term; if something is as good as done or will be done very easily it is said to be “duck soup”.

With this in mind, Fitzpatrick visited 43 artists’ studios and chose a single non-art object. The assembly of these curiosities creates an intriguing incidental menagerie – a most unusual and innocently intimate group portrait is the result of this quirky collection – each item becoming a talisman for the others.
