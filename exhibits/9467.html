HomeBase IV

323 East Broadway
323 East Broadway, near Clinton Street
East Village / Lower East Side
May 9 - May 24, 2009
Reception: Saturday, May 9, 1 - 8 PM
Web Site

HomeBase IV takes place this year in a former medical center in the Lower East Side. This year’s artists include award winning playwright David Bar Katz (US), installation artist Willum Geertz (Holland), photographer Oded Hirsch (Israel), installation artist Sandra Lee (Korean-American), artist Pessi Margulies (Israel), performance artist J .Morrison (US), photographer Paul Sepuya (US), artist Dafna Shalom (Israel), and artist Letha Wilson (US). Special projects by guest artists and HomeBase team members.

www.homebaseproject.com

HomeBase IV Program Saturday May 9th 1:00 Site tour 4:00 Salon Conversation “Art in the Time of Cholera” with Christian Viveros -Faune (VOLTA NY), Kipton Cronkite (Kipton Art) and HomeBase Founder and Artistic Director Anat Litwin 5:30 House warming Party featuring Music Performance by Isra-Alien: a journey into the roots of Israeli music through international musical influences such as Greek, European, Gypsy, Russian and American Jazz, Blues and Rock. 7:00 – 8:00 HB performances

Sunday May 10th 1:00 Site Tour 1:30 A conversation about the Educational Alliance History as reflection of the changes in the Lower East Side Lynn Appelbaum, Chief Program Officer of the Educational Alliance 2:00 Poetry Reading by Calypso Muse: Curated by Cheryl Boyce-Taylor Featuring: Rodlyn Douglas, Marjorie Tesser. Ekere Tallie, Marta Lucia, Cheryl Boyce-Taylor. Celebrating Mothers Day 4:00 Salon Conversation with HomeBase IV NY artist Pessi Margulies 5:00 -7:00 HB Performances
