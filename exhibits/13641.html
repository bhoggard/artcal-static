ESP TV #1 Viewing Party

LOUIS V E.S.P. at Angels and Kings
500 East 11th Street, 732-552-7199
East Village / Lower East Side
April 27 - April 27, 2011
Reception: Wednesday, April 27, 8 - 12 AM
Web Site

NOTE: THIS LOUIS V E.S.P. EVENT DOES NOT TAKE PLACE AT LOUIS V E.S.P. HQ, IT IS AN ESPTV PARTY AT ANGELS AND KINGS, 500 E 11TH ST NY NY

The inaugural episode of ESP TV airs on Manhattan Neighborhood Network on April 27th at 9:30PM on Channel 57 (Time Warner) / 83 (RCN). Come gather around 11 TVs with us and watch it happen live through the magic of public television. Sure you could watch it online later, but wouldn’t you rather enjoy the communal feeling that only happens with sports events, political debates or major disasters, of crowds watching a dying medium together in a bar?

This is the start of a regular TV showcase of NYC based artists produced by Louis V E.S.P. made possible by help from our friends at Vaudeville Park. Each episode is taped “in front of a live studio audience” with live primitive video effects processing and a rotating lineup of illustrious hosts.

For these “TV Parties”, Ian M. Colletti (Vaudeville Park) will be taking over the bar while Scott Kiernan and David McDaniel (Totally Wired, Life After Breakfast) DJ throughout the night. Artist editions will be on display. Live performances for each night to be announced soon. The bar has been kind enough to let us totally “take over” on these nights.

Episode #2 screens the following Wednesday, May 4th at 9:30 on channel 56 (Time Warner) / 83 (RCN).

For those that cannot make it out, the show will be available online after each screening and on VHS editions of 100.

Episodes 1 and 2 feature performances by: Ganjatronics Elbis Rever Kunsole Rachel Mason Sam Mickens w/ “The Dust Up” Dana Bell w/ Kerry Davis and Leah Retherford

w/ your hosts Bradford Nordeen as “Mary Boome” and Hayley Blatte as “Coco”

with videos by: Brian Zegeer, Erica Magrey, Sophia Peer, Derek Larson, Colby Bird, Jennifer Sullivan and Andrew Steinmetz, Jonathan Phelps, Kate Gilmore, Andre Perkowski, and Katrina Lamb

http://www.louisvesp.com for more info

and be there for the second taping on May 6th at The Schoolhouse in Brooklyn!
