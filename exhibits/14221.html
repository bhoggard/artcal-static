ESP TV #4 Screening party at K &M Bar, 225 N 8th St., Brooklyn NY July 28, 8-2

Episode #4 of ESP TV airs Tuesday, May 28th on at 9:30 on channel 57 (Time Warner) / 84 (RCN) / Manhattan Neighborhood Network 3.

Featuring performances by: INNERGAZE with videos by: Shana Moulton, Aaron Nemec, Karen Y. Chan, Jennifer Sullivan and Andrew Steinmetz, Team Louis V E.S.P. and more!

With your host Sam Mickens (Sam Mickens Ecstatic Showband Revue, The Dead Science, Xiu Xiu)

Come gather around a huge tv with us and watch it happen live through the magic of public television. Sure you could watch it online later, but wouldn’t you rather enjoy the communal feeling that only happens with sports events, political debates or major disasters, of crowds watching a dying medium together in a bar?

This is a regular TV showcase of NYC based artists produced by Louis V E.S.P. made possible by help from our friends at Vaudeville Park. Each episode is taped “in front of a live studio audience” with live primitive video effects processing and a rotating lineup of illustrious hosts.

DJ sets by Innergaze and LVESP.

For those that cannot make it out, the show will be available online after each screening and on VHS editions of 100.

Produced for Manhattan Neighborhood Network by Louis V E.S.P.
