A Fold in the Fabric

LMAKprojects at MonkeyTown
58 North 3rd Street, between Kent and Wythe, 718-384-1369
Williamburg
September 16 - October 13, 2006
Reception: Saturday, September 16, 5 - 7 PM
Web Site

LMAKprojects opens A Fold in the Fabric, its season-long media performance, screening, and exhibition series, with an evening of media works at the incomparable MonkeyTown venue in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. A Fold in the Fabric will showcase artists working along the axis between media and material. These artists explore the interpenetration of live presence, mediated experience and objectness through works of film, sound, video, installation and media performance.

MonkeyTown’s unique environment, a theater in the round in a room built of images, provides us with the opportunity to explore this material-media axis from an exciting vantage. Here, light and image become architectural building materials for space and structure, while sound can project planes of force as well as conjure sonorous objects. Shifting levels of presence and deferral, as well as real and virtual space, are folded and woven together into an intricate tapestry of experience.

Program:

  • Chika and Zach Layton, live video and electronic sound.
  • Jill Magid, Kafkahouse
  • Mathilde Rosier, Lac
  • sawako, Floating, live sound and video.
  • Vincent Grenier, Tabula Rasa
  • Seoungho Cho, Untitled
  • Willum Geerts, video and live sound performance
