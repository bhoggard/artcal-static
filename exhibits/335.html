Produced at Eyebeam: Work in Process

PICK

Eyebeam
540 West 21st Street, 718.222.3982
Chelsea
January 20 - March 5, 2005
Reception: Thursday, January 20, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Produced at Eyebeam: Works In Process, is part one of two exhibitions featuring recent work from Eyebeam’s 2004 Artists in Residence, Moving Image Studio Commission, Co-op and Fellowship Programs. Like software released in versions, many of the installations in Works in Process are iterative and participatory, changing for and by the user. This exhibition also embodies the atelier-oriented approach or process over projects cultivated in Eyebeamâ€™s studios. Work in the exhibition explores interactive global streaming, the architecture of democracy, body modification and corporate branding, aerial surveillance and gaming, pirate broadcasting, and the impact of technology on highly personal expression.

Public Programs (all public programs will run from 6:30-8:30pm): January 26: An evening of tours and talks with the Works In Process artists February 17: Urban gaming forum moderated by Jenny Marketo February 25: Pirate Radio panel organized by neuroTransmitter March 3: Presentation of Girls-Eye View 2004 program projects with Mariam Ghani

Exhibiting artists include:

Beeoff Knowear Mariam Ghani Jenny Marketou neuroTransmitter Bec Stupak
