Boys Don’t Cry

Baang and Buren Contemporary
548 West 28th Street, Suite 238
Chelsea
October 4 - November 8, 2012
Reception: Thursday, October 4, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Baang and Burne Contemporary is estatic about announcing Boys Don’t Cry, a three person exhibition showcasing our new talent: Chris Jehly, Rich Tu, and Joseph Cultice.

These three artists stemming from different disciplines, working in different mediums come together in Boys Don’t Cry. Finding a pathway to express their desires, fears, and unanswerable questions poignantly through their art are what each artist has in common. By utilizing personal demons stuck within, each unfolds the inner workings of their psyche earnestly, explosively, sarcastically, violently and with hope.

The Opening Reception for Boys Don’t Cry begins Thursday October 4th from 6-8pm at 547 W28th St. Between 10th and 11th Ave.

The show is provocative and beautiful. We cant wait to share it with you. Please join us for the opening reception on Oct 4th from 6-8p!
