Excavation: Material/Memory/Artifact

Ed. Varie (East 7th Street)
208 East 7th Street
East Village / Lower East Side
February 25 - March 1, 2012
Reception: Saturday, February 25, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

Opening Reception Feb 18, 25 & March 3rd, 6-9pm

Ed Varie is pleased to present EXCAVATION: MATERIAL/MEMORY/ARTIFACT a group exhibition in 3-parts, featuring works by 30 artists of the Pratt Institute M.F.A. department with works ranging from photography and sculpture to performance.

Artist include: Felix Aarts, Leonidas Chalepas, Kyoungah Choi, I-Hsuen Chen, Alex Crawford, Cristina de Miguel, Deshawn Dumas, Jean Paul Gomez, Johanna Hamm, Jill Hochkiss, Meredith Hoffheins, Raul Hott, Levi Jackson, Julie Janicek, June Kim, Megan Liu Kincheloe, Tao Kulczycki, Kelly McCafferty, Nick Naber, Austin Nelson, Lucia Oceguera, Matthew Pisacano, Anthony Palocci, Patrick Rowe, Hiba Schahbaz, Judith Shimer, Anna Sorenson, Karla Stingerstein, Ian Swanson, Aderyn Wood.

Curated by Lindsay Aveilhe on behalf of Pratt Artists League.

Excavation examines the varied use and role of excavation in todayâ€™s art. The exhibition will be conceptualized in three categories, each one succinct. The first exhibition, Material, will include artists whose practice involves the physical excavation of their medium, the second, Memory, will include artists who seek to mine and uncover fragments of a personal or a collective memory, and the last installment, Artifact, includes artists whose work seeks to materialize, reconstruct, or systemize artifacts or the object(s) of investigation.
