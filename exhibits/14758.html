Perfect Man II – Curated by Rita Ackermann and Parinaz Mogadassi

White Columns
320 West 13th Street, Entrance on Horatio Street between 8th Avenue and Hudson Street, 212-924-4212
Chelsea
September 10 - October 15, 2011
Reception: Friday, September 9, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Perfect man can kill a tarantula with a broom in a businesslike manner, half-afraid.

Perfect man is the tarantula and the broom.

BAS JAN ADER KAI ALTHOFF ANTONIN R. ARTAUD BERNADETTE CORPORATION ANTOINE CATALA PETER DOIG MIKLÓS ERDÉLY LLYN FOULKES DAN GRAHAM HANK HIVNOR ROMEO KLEIN MARKUS LÜPERTZ JOHN MAYBURY MALCOLM MORLEY KEN OKIISHI ED PASCHKE RAMMELLZEE RICHARD SERRA JOSH SMITH TROJAN DANIEL TURNER DON VAN VLIET

White Columns is proud to present ‘Perfect Man II’ an exhibition curated by Rita Ackermann and Parinaz Mogadassi. In a statement accompanying the exhibition Ackermann and Mogadassi state:

“The focus of this exhibition is on those men who are on a quest, on the cold and sharpest blade of a knife, represented by their personal studies of construction and their struggle to defeat the elements that society’s subculture dictates with its common sense of aesthetic mediocrity (designer art). They are ruled by the laws of self-seeing the subject of modern art only through the unique mind of the individual. The conquest can be destructive and useless but it leads to a complete knowledge of himself. Brutal fragility in order to be untouchable, free of references and without compromises but with the resolve of a hard fisted clarity.”

A series of events, including readings, film screenings and a book launch will accompany the exhibition, details to be announced shortly.
