Reunited Boston University School of Visual Arts Alumni

Denise Bibro Fine Art
529 West 20th Street, 4th Floor, 212-647-7030
Chelsea
February 18 - March 13, 2010
Reception: Friday, March 5, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Denise Bibro Fine Art is pleased to present Reunited, showcasing 17 alumni of Boston University’s College of Fine Arts.

Part of CFA’s InCite Arts Festival, this exhibition features painting and sculpting alumni from the Boston University School of Visual Arts, one of three schools within the College of Fine Arts. An artists’ talk will be held on Friday, March 5, from 5-6pm.

Since its launch in 1954, the BU School of Visual Arts has nurtured scores of accomplished and renowned contemporary artists including painters Brice Marden and Pat Steir, and dealer/gallery owner Arnold Glimcher.

Artists include:

Sachiko Akiyama William Augustus Shira Avidor Andrea Bergart Michelle Bova Jonathan Daly Alfredo Gisholt Jonathon Jackson Carol Keller Sarah Meder Marc Mitchell Lydia Musco Kayla Mohammadi Matt Phillips Gabriel Phipps Russell Roberts Aithan Shapira Lisa Taliano Lindsey Warren
