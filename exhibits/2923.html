10 Curatorial Perspectives

PICK

Haven Arts
50 Bruckner Boulevard, Bldg. A, 718-585-5753
Bronx
September 4 - September 29, 2006
Reception: Friday, September 8, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

10 Curatorial Perspectives is an exhibition showcasing the selections of ten different curators, who each control a designated wall of the gallery space. They are: Carey Clark, Edwin Gonzalez, Barry Kostrinsky, Anat Litwin, Aristides Logothetis, Wayne Northcross, J.C. Rice, Tim Rollins, Nathan Schrieber, and Zimad. The range of expertise brought to bear by these diverse curators and their wide acquaintance with artists and other galleries enriches this collaboration.
