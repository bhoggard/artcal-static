The Studio Visit

PICK

Exit Art
475 Tenth Avenue, corner 36th Street, 212-966-7745
Hell's Kitchen
January 7 - January 28, 2006
Reception: Saturday, January 7, 7 - 10 PM
Web Site

The Studio Visit exposes more than 160 artists’ workspaces through short videos created by each of the artists. Seven multidisciplinary artists will work in Exit Art’s storefront windows for the duration of the show, and several will offer interactive workshops to gallery visitors. Finally, each Thursday, Friday and Saturday (except January 14) a line up of emerging poetry and music talent will perform at 8 p.m. with hot and hearty soup warming up audiences before the shows at 7 p.m.
