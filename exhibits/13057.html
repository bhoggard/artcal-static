Josh Tonsfeldt was born in Independence, Missouri and lives and works in New York. He completed his MFA at Columbia University in 2007. Recent solo exhibitions include ‘4:Cat-Cos’ at Franco Soffiantino Gallery in Turin in 2010. He was recently included in group exhibitions at the SculptureCenter, The White Columns Annual, curated by Bob Nickas, and The Artist’s Institute. in New York He is also included in the current exhibition ‘Proposal for a Floor’, curated by Alex Gartenfeld, for the Zabludowicz Collection in New York.
