AUX FOLLIES : Improper Residency

IN RIVERS...
165 Greenpoint Avenue, STOREFRONT, 718-383-0311
Greenpoint
May 6 - May 8, 2011
Reception: Friday, May 6, 7 - 10 PM
Web Site

For the past Three weeks, six artists have been working and producing art work in our space, two remotely, resulting in collaborative ideas and art works from 8 artist hailing from various backgrounds and specializing in different mediums.

From Friday May 6th – Sunday May 8th we will celebrate with a culmination exhibition that will include installations, paintings, sculpture, music and much more. Please join us all weekend for the seriousness of having fun!

Detailed Schedule: FRI MAY 6TH OPENING RECEPTION 7-10PM – Drinks, Snacks Music SAT MAY 7TH ONGOING EVENTS 12-10PM – Performance events SUN MAY 8TH CLOSING RECEPTION 6-9PM – Drinks, Snacks Music
