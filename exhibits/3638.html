Eva and Franco Mattes, 13 Most Beautiful Avatars

The Italian Academy, Columbia University
1161 Amsterdam Avenue, 212-854-2306
Harlem
November 30 - December 19, 2006
Reception: Thursday, November 30, 6 - 8 PM
Web Site

Curated by Marisa Olson

13 Most Beautiful Avatars, an exhibition in Second Life’s increasingly popular Ars Virtua gallery -a virtual nonprofit arts organization- captures the most visually dynamic and celebrated “stars” of Second Life. The Matteses have been living in the virtual world, Second Life, for over a year, exploring its terrain and interacting with its peculiar inhabitants. The result of their “video-game flanerie” is this series of portraits.

The 13 Most Beautiful Avatars images will be exhibited, in the real-world, at the Italian Academy at New York’s Columbia University. For the Ars Virtua show, in Second Life, a 3D replica of this physical exhibition space has been recreated for presentation of an exhibition identical to the “real” one.
