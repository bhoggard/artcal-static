Without giving away too much, weâ€™d like to invite you to the newest iteration of SURPRISE!!!, a performance event, group exhibition and zine release party on July 21, from 7 – 10pm.

SURPRISE!!! is a recurring, roving performance project, created in 2009 by artists Aaron Wojack and Calder Zwicky. Each SURPRISE!!! event is a site-specific occurrence, taking place in vastly different environments including art galleries, boats, tents, and more. Coinciding with the performance event will be a retrospective group exhibition featuring three years of photography, ephemera, and artwork by collaborators and zine contributors: Aaron Anderson, Gabe Dikel, Keith Mendak and Angelo Zwicky. In addition, we will be releasing two brand new issues of SURPRISE!!! Each performance is a catalyst for the next issue, so photographs from this event will be published in the next issue of SURPRISE!!! along with a new selection of guest artworks.

More info is available at www.gazillionmillion.com.

Please join us Saturday, July 21 from 7 – 10pm, for an opening party + zine release for issues #5 and #6. The SURPRISE!!! event will be held from 8 – 9pm.

SURPRISE!!! is the eleventh in a series of group exhibitions dedicated to providing self-publishing artists, who generally share their work through printed matter and other ephemeral media, with a platform for exhibition, experimentation and exploration outside of the printed format, organized by Aimee Lusty.

Artwork + Editions available online here: http://booklynartgallery.bigcartel.com/
