Bushwick Schlacht! Germany vs. USA – Painting Pop Up Show

PICK

245 Boerum Street
245 Boerum Street
Bushwick/Ridgewood
April 3 - April 4, 2010
Reception: Saturday, April 3, 6 - 10 PM
Web Site

Curated by Guillermo Creus with Tom Sanford and Marcel Hüppauff

The German title of the show Bushwick Schlach! (Bushwick Battle!), refers to the original conception for the show: a battle/collaboration between painters with the idea of creating interrelations between each individual work, sourced from different aesthetics but within a similar medium. Like a DJ battle, that creates sound from action and reaction and so forth, this painting battle would create new visual experiences through addition and accumulation.
