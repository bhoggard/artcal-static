Salon du Jour

PICK

Autoversion, LTD
453 West 17th Street, 4th floor, 212-242-0060
Chelsea
May 2 - June 7, 2008
Reception: Friday, May 2, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

Please join us at AUTOVERSION LTD. Gallery, NYC for the debut of NO MILK TODAY vol.#1 – and a special one-day-only salon show of original artwork, multi-media performances, videos, munchies,and extra special surprise guests!

Come see and hear what awaits you in our labyrinth of mystery and wonder. Thrill to sights & sounds never witnessed in this dimension. You’ll feel your cares slip away and experience an inner joy and sense of satisfaction you never dreamed possible (or at least you’ll feel good coz you had a fun time for free in NYC)!

LIVE multi-media performances by: -Rose Kallal -Rachel Mason -The Spirit of the Chamber

SECRET ART, featuring: Jonathan Berger AA Bronson Kathe Burkhart Katarina Burin Reuben Cox Pia Dehne Peter Eide Carl Ferrero Janine Gordon Paul Moreno Mike Nolan Jason Seder Paul Sepuya Christian Siekmeier Nico Urquiza Jan Wandrag Julia Weist & Special Guests!...
