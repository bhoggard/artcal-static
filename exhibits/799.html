Mikiya Matsuda, Foreplay

ATM (20th Street)
511 West 20th Street, 212-375-0349
Chelsea
June 3 - July 9, 2005
Reception: Friday, June 3, 6 - 9 PM

A solo exhibition of new paintings by Mikiya Matsuda, whose works ATM last presented in Painting, 2002.

Matsuda plays upon the fanciful metaphors of luxury home furnishing lines by American and French designers Donna Karan, Ralph Lauren and Yves Delorme. The title installation, “Foreplay,” will re-arrange weekly.
