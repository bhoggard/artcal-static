Yoko Motomiya, Windflow

Vlepo
36 Richmond Terrace, 718-442-0762
Staten Island
July 5 - July 30, 2005
Reception: Saturday, July 9, 3 - 6 PM
Web Site

Ms. Motomiya’s silk screened prints and installations are influenced by the aesthetics of technologies past, present and an imagined future. Printed on surfaces ranging from traditional paper forms to vellum, vinyl, plastic and tile. The pieces stand alone or aggregate into clusters revealing a highly aestheticized approach to interior environments.

The gallery is located directly across the street from the Staten Island Ferry Terminal.
