Opening Reception: Friday, February 11 from 7:00 -10:00pm

The Hogar Collection is most proud to present, To Be Continued…, a group exhibition of the gallery’s artists celebrating 8 years of business and a close to it’s present incarnation. For over 8 years the gallery has claimed it’s stake as a staple in the Williamsburg and international art scene and has been a determined and proactive force in some of the best emerging American and International art. Championing innovative, intelligent and challenging artworks has always been the focus of the gallery’s program and I am very happy to have some of the most talented artists that I know working on my team. The exhibition will highlight some of the great works shown over the years and will serve as a milestone marking of our accomplishments. Included in the exhibition will be works by Lauriston Avery, Cecilia Biagini, Michael Behle, Lee Ranaldo, Dave Choi, Michelle Forsyth, Damian Catera, Jihoon Park, JJ Garfinkel, Miki Lee, Matt Wycoff, Walter Markham, Juan Jose Cambre, Florencia Bohtlingk, Martin Gurfein, Rafael Bueno and Martin Reyna.
