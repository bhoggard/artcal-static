The Modernists

RL Fine Arts
39 West 19th Street, Suite 612, 212-645-6402
Chelsea
July 22 - August 30, 2010
Web Site

RL Fine Arts is pleased to present a group show of some of India’s most important senior Modernist artists.

The Modernist period of Indian art is most often taken to refer to the artists who came of age during and after the turbulent times of Indian Independence and then throughout the 1950’s, 60’s, 70’s. The most famous artists of this period are the Progressive Artist group, which was a short lived group that came into existence after independence but which included some of the most famous names in Indian art.

Artists:

Arpita Singh Jehangir Sabavala Francis Newton Souza Maqbool Fida Husain Syed Haider Raza
