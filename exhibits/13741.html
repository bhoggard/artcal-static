Working together.

SUGAR Gallery
449 Troutman Street, 718-417-1180
Bushwick/Ridgewood
April 30 - June 11, 2011
Reception: Saturday, April 30, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

SUGAR is pleased to present Working together.

Peter Fagundo Doug Young

The likelihood of explaining this exhibition in a nut shell, so as to understand the pairing of these two artists artworks, is unlikely. As Rudi Fuchs was once overheard saying before an exhibition “You can’t explain art. A museum is about comparison.” Though SUGAR is not a museum, and I am not a curator, the discovery of Rudi Fuchs and his endeavors has given me the confidence to continue my “uneven exhibitions”, which I prefer to be known as installations.

This installation was founded on one request: “I want to show with Pete”. SUGAR is not a collaborative project to those lengths, it might even be a stretch of the imagination. However, the admiration and respect I have for both artists is not beyond any reach and it is their loyalty and devotion to their own process, practice and vision- that make up the sole proprietorship of Working together.
