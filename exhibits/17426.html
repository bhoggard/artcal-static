CALENDAR | HOSTING

Pratt Alumni Painters

Pratt Manhattan Gallery
144 West 14th Street, (at 7th Avenue), 212 647-7778
Chelsea
June 8 - July 28, 2012
Reception: Thursday, June 7, 7 - 9 PM
Web Site

Pratt Manhattan Gallery will present “Pratt Alumni Painters,” an exhibition of work by 14 emerging and established painters who graduated from Pratt within the last three decades, from June 8 through July 28, 2012, at 144 West 14th Street, second floor.

The work of Pratt alumni has been displayed at many prominent New York museums including The Museum of Modern Art, The Metropolitan Museum of Art, and the Brooklyn Museum, as well as at museums and galleries internationally. This exhibition, which is curated by Nick Battis, Director of Exhibitions, Pratt Institute, will be celebrated with an opening reception on Thursday, June 7 from 7 to 9 PM. The exhibition and opening reception are free and open to the public.

The full list of participating alumni artists includes:

Derrick Adams (B.F.A. Art and Design Education, 1996)

Polina Barskaya (M.F.A. Painting/Drawing, 2010)

Trudy Benson (M.F.A. Painting/Drawing, 2010)

Matthew Deleget (Combined M.S./M.F.A., 1997)

Elaine Komorowski (M.F.A. Painting, 1990)

Il Lee (M.F.A. Painting, 1982)

Carrie Moyer (B.F.A. Painting, 1985)

Lisa Sanditz (M.F.A. Painting, 2001)

Kris Scheifele (M.F.A. Painting, 2009)

Andrew Sendor (B.F.A. Painting, 2000)

Russell Tyler (M.F.A. Painting, 2010)

Takashi Usui (M.F.A. Painting, 1996)

Marc Van Cauwenbergh (M.F.A. Painting, 1989)

Blade Wynne (B.F.A. Painting, 2002)
