Silent Auction and Shop: Summer 2011

July 1 - July 31, 2011
Reception: Friday, July 1, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

For Immediate Release:

AG Gallery 107-A North 3rd Street Brooklyn, NY 11211

Announces, “Silent Auction and Shop: Summer 2011”

July 1st, Fri – July 31st, Sun Opening Night: July 1st, 2011, 6-9pm

AG Gallery in Williamsburg is pleased to present an fundraiser event, “Silent Auction and Shop: Summer 2011,” with new artists from open call and passionate AG Gallery artists to support the people especially who suffered from the massive earthquake on March 2011 by the power of art lovers.  

Thankfully, more than 20 artists show/donate beautiful art pieces for a silent auction and shop at AG Gallery for Japan relief. Not only the artists who have exhibited AG Gallery before, but also new talented artists show each work such as a painting, photograph, collage, and more for a silent auction and shop which will take place from July 1st to July 31st, 2011.

As we will not close bidding at one night, you could join the bidding anytime during this one month period of event, also you are welcome to come in and enjoy the artworks any time during this period.  In the shop section, you could buy and take home original works of art donated by passionate artists. Come out, show your support and have fun.

Bids will start at around $10 and up (depends on the original price: those who just have to have it can “BUY NOW” for its original price.)

The participated art pieces will be auctioned or sold, and all the proceeds will be donated to the American Red Cross to helping people who need a support from others including people in Japan who are trying to recover from the massive disaster.

Honored artists include:      Cristina Rose, B5000ART PROJECT, William Crump, Ben Knight, Marileea Järnefelt, Shoko Miki, Betty Doll, Daniel Vennemann, Mary Bucci McCoy, Lin Bao-Ling, Maureen Shields, Elizabeth Riccardi, Elaine Chow, Jinta Hasegawa, Coral Silverman, Ken Hus, Alexis Duque, Jen Burkin, Thomas Parker Williams, Martha Nilsson Edelheit, Karen Fitzgelard, Paula Overbay, Peter Janssen, and more…

The opening night will be on July 1st at About Glamour/AG Gallery from 6 to 9 p.m. The event will be take place through July 31st, 2011.

Contact/Appointment: Takeshi Iida AG Gallery [email protected] www.aboutglamour.net
