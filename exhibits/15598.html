Holiday Book Bazaar

Aperture Foundation
547 West 27th Street, 4th Floor, 212-505-5555
Chelsea
December 10 - December 10, 2011
Web Site

Aperture invites you to join us for our first Holiday Book Bazaar. In addition to Aperture, the following publishers will host booths during this daylong event:

ARTBOOK | DAP Capricious Conveyor Arts The Ice Plant Je Suis une Bande de Jeunes Magnum Pau Wau Publications

There will be special programming throughout the day, including: books signings with Kathy Ryan, photo editor of the New York Times Magazine, and photographer Alex Webb; a book raffle; a book donation “giving tree”; and much more! Holiday refreshments will also be available.
