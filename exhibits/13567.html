Creative Time Spring Gala Art Auction Preview at Phillips de Pury and Artnet.com

Leading up to Creative Time’s annual Spring Gala on May 5, selected works from the Gala Auction will be previewed in an exhibition at Phillips de Pury & Company’s downtown location, 450 West 15th Street in New York, from Friday, April 8 to Wednesday, April 20. The works in the exhibition, including pieces by Yoko Ono, Cindy Sherman, Swoon, Aaron Young, and Louise Lawler, among others, is open to bids from the public via Creative Time’s Event and Membership Associate Director Danielle Schmidt ([email protected]).

Artnet.com will also feature highlights from the auction from April 20 through May 3, available for the public to bid on or purchase directly. Artnet has generously waived its usual online fees for Creative Time auction bidders, making it even easier to place bids for your favorite works.

WHAT: Creative Time Spring Gala Art Auction Preview

*WHERE: Phillips de Pury & Company 450 West 15th Street New York, NY 10011

WHEN: Friday, April 8 – Wednesday, April 20, 2011 Monday to Friday, 10am – 6pm

WHO: Artwork by Yoko Ono, Cindy Sherman, Swoon, Anne Collier, Aaron Young, Louise Lawler, Orly Genger, Hernan Bas, Olaf Breuning, and Wayne Gonzales

CONTACT: Danielle Schmidt, Associate Director, Events and Membership [email protected], 212.206.6674×212
