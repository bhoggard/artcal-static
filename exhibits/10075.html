Olaf Breuning Nancy de Holl Martha Friedman Rachel Mason Fabian Marti Michele O’Marah Mamiko Otsubo Michael Queenland Michael Rashkow Matthew Spiegelman

Offset Project is a new not-for-profit arts endeavor. Posters are approximately 39” x 27” in size. The price of each poster is $100.

200 posters of each artwork have been produced, with half the run given to the artist and the other half used for pop-up exhibitions and for fundraising.

OFFSET had its inaugural exhibition at Mount Tremper Arts in NY’s Hudson Valley. Future exhibitions are planned for cities across the US and in Europe.

Exhibition hours: Thursday, September 24, to Saturday, September 26, from 11am to 6pm.

For more information please visit www.offset-project.com or e-mail [email protected]
