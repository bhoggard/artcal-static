site95 Benefit

Present Company
101 North 13th Street
Williamburg
July 19 - July 26, 2012
Reception: Thursday, July 26, 6 - 9 PM
Web Site

site95 will present an exhibition benefit with over 80 artists. Taking place on July 26th, from 6 to 9 pm, the benefit captures the spirited feeling of our “Dead in August” series, a lively collection of cultural and artistic events for the public when the rest of New York has closed down and taken the typical August summer vacation. Over 80 artists have donated small-scale works including works on paper, paintings, photographs, and sculptures. The exhibition will be on view at Present Company in Brooklyn, New York and reproduced in the August site95 Journal. Ticket holders will take home a piece featured in the benefit. Along with providing a platform for local artists to display their work, the benefit will help site95 raise funds to cover exhibition expenses.
