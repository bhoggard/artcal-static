SFAQ – NYC Release party for issue 11

Participant Inc.
253 East Houston Street, 212-254-4334
East Village / Lower East Side
November 17 - November 17, 2012
Reception: Saturday, November 17, 4 - 6 PM
Web Site

SFAQ (San Francisco Arts Quarterly) International Arts and Culture www.sfaqonline.com

SFAQ Contact:  Andrew McClintock [email protected]

SFAQ is pleased to announce an informal free release party for Issue 11, which includes feature interviews from some of NYC’s finest: Vito Acconci, Jonas Mekas, Tom Sachs, Paula Cooper, Mathew Higgs, Kembra Pfahler, Carolee Schneemann, Brett Littman and Nayland Blake.

Please join SFAQ’s Founders & Editors at PARTICIPANT INC. (253 E. Houston St.) on Saturday, November 17th, from 4 – 6:30pm. We invite you to pick up a free copy of SFAQ, enjoy the complementary beverages and view the gallery’s solo exhibition  from Vaginal Davis.

SFAQ is a free quarterly publication based out of the Tenderloin district in San Francisco, CA. It features both domestic and international, historical and contemporary arts and culture. Distributed free to fourteen countries around the world, SFAQ aims to bridge the gap created by academic “high-art” publications by focusing on the implications of art in a broader social context. 

We hope to see you there!!
