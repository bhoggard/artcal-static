I am working on art activity mainly about two dimensional art works in Japan. What I draw about is the view which does not exist as a real particular place, but I wish that exists in somewhere. The image could be the description of me like a self-portrait. I depict pictures about the words or emotion that I want to express to the world by putting color on something from the inside of my mind. The thing from my mind makes me create a basic form of image, and focus on its colors. Then, the image changes to the view of somewhere which I believe the existence. This is my challenge to connect to peoples’ mind emotionally by using common language, art.
