Victor Reyes & Steel MSK “All Write You Scumbags”

Klughaus Gallery
47 Monroe Street, 646-801-6024
East Village / Lower East Side
September 14 - September 29, 2012
Reception: Friday, September 14, 6 - 10 PM
Web Site

Klughaus Gallery and LRG present “All Write You Scumbags,” a dual artist show featuring recent works by Victor REYES and STEEL. “All Write You Scumbags” marks the New York debut for both Bay Area-based artists and showcases a distinct chemistry culled over years working together as friends, creative partners, and members of MSK, one of the highest regarded graffiti artist collectives at the moment.

Victor REYES has been painting since the early 90’s. His work has been shown extensively in countries all across the world. “These new works are painted and illustrated as a reveal to the contemporary ideas surrounding graffiti and its application within the conversation of fine art,” says Victor of the duo’s Klughaus show.

STEEL is an artist from San Francisco who paints cheeseburgers. He has a great appreciation for classic print design and sign painting. He is inspired by his friends, Richard Pryor, and good food.
