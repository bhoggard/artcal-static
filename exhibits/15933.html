Reception: Saturday, January 14, 8 - 10 PM
Web Site

Video Vaudeville Presents… “Channel to Channel”   Saturday January 14th, 8 – 10 pm FREE EVENT   Vaudeville Park 26 Bushwick Avenue Brooklyn, NY www.vaudevillepark.org   In conjunction with the US screening premiere and cable access broadcast of Ode on a Terran Urn by Erica Magrey, Vaudeville Park presents Channel to Channel, a video program curated by Magrey.   Channel to Channel includes works that are influenced by the formats, genres, themes, and conventions of television shows or the experience of consuming them. Broad in scope and intention, this selection of videos tackles the tropes of situation comedy, science fiction, talk shows, cartoons, and instructional TV as well as considering the spaces in front of and inside the screen.   Featuring the work of:   Erica Magrey Jennifer Sullivan Sophia Peer Rachael Morrison Frankie Martin Myles Langlois Jennifer Juniper Stratford Brina Thurston John Kilduff Ben Dowell

The TV episode will be aired Tuesday, 1/10 on Channel 67, Time Warner cable in Manhattan, MNN network.
