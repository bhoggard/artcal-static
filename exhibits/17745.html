Endless

266 W. 37th St. (between 7th and 8th Ave.) New York, NY

July 19 – 22

Gallery Hours, Reception and Events: Tuesday, July 17, live installation 12 – 6pm Wednesday, July 18, live installation 12 – 6pm Thursday, July 19, 12 – 6pm; opening reception 6 – 9pm Friday, July 20, 12 – 6pm; performance and reception 6 – 10pm Saturday, July 21, performances 10am – 10pm Sunday, July 22, 10am – 2pm

“Endless features the work of six contemporary artists working in new media and sculpture. As we all represent the generation of the Millennial, digital experience is inherently related to any or all of our work.

The artist collaboration in the space will address the questions of over saturated media, digitally mediated experiences, and question in general what authentic experience is today.”

Exhibiting Artists: Anthony Tino, Audrey Ryan, Joe Kewin, Kevin Dejewski, Omar Soliman, Paul John
